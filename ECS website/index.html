How to enjoy Hollow Knight

Hollow knight is a very fun game but it does take some time and paitence.

Once you give it time it becomes a really fun game (and maybe your favorite :3)

Now, what are the steps?

The very beginning of the game is very boring and simple, but don't let that discourage you!

Now once you get past the tutorial, you'll find yourself in a dark, gloomy, and nearly deserted town called Dirtmouth, but remember this is just the beginning!
